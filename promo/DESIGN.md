## Style Prompt

Dark terminal aesthetic — the composition should feel like looking into an active developer terminal with a polished, cinematic overlay. Deep navy-black canvas. Teal accent for interactive elements, warm amber for highlights and scores. Monospace typography for terminal recreation, condensed display font for titles. Motion should feel precise and mechanical — snappy entrances, tabular data appearing row by row, progress bars filling smoothly.

## Colors

- `#0B1622` — Canvas background (deep navy-black)
- `#15293E` — Card/panel backgrounds (slightly lifted)
- `#2DD4BF` — Primary accent (teal — interactive elements, selected items)
- `#F59E0B` — Warm accent (amber — scores, progress bars, highlights)
- `#E2E8F0` — Primary text (cool white)
- `#64748B` — Secondary text (slate gray — descriptions, dims)

## Typography

- **Headlines:** Space Grotesk — geometric display, 800 weight
- **Terminal/body:** JetBrains Mono — monospace, 400 weight

## What NOT to Do

- No gradient text or neon glow effects — this is a serious developer tool, not a gaming promo
- No rounded corners on terminal panels — sharp edges only
- No emoji animations — emoji appears as static text in the terminal recreation
- No generic sans-serif body text — everything in the terminal should be monospace
- No slow fades — entrances should be snappy (0.2-0.4s), matching terminal responsiveness
